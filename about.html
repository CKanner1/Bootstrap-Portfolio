Currently, I work in a family business with my father and brother-in-law. Before working in the family business, I graduated from Babson College in Boston. Babson College is a small, 1,400 U-Grad college offering degrees only in business. They are best known for their studies in entrepreneurship and have been ranked #1 in that subject for 21 consecutive years by US News and World Report.

After college, I started and ran several of my own companies. The most successful of which is called Alpha Architect. Alpha Architect is a registered investment advisor that markets and sells our proprietary quantitative investment strategies. Alpha Architect goes to market through separately managed accounts, a traditional hedge fund product and through our five publically traded exchange-traded funds.

Our company Pubco, and through its subsidiaries manufactures and markets pressure sensitive labels, mobile label printers, industrial demolition equipment and typewriters. At my current job, I am responsible for new acquisition, sourcing equipment and helping to oversee our manufacturing operation.
